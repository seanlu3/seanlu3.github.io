About Us

From providing communities the tools for creating an additional food source to being at the forefront of advocating for the universal right to food, Seed aims to collaborate with food banks and community food programs by helping them become more self-sufficient by transforming under-itilized spaces into community gardens.

Our Vision

End hunger in Ontario

Our Mission

We are teaching Ontario the science of horticulture by transforming underutilized spaces into edible landscapes to provide food banks and community food programs a self-sufficient way increasing access to nutritional food sources.
