Build-Your-Own:

Step 1: Choose Your Leafy Greens: lettuce, spinach, or kale

Step 2: Choose Your Crops: beans, radish, or onions

Step 3: Choose Your Herbs: rosemary, thyme, basil, or parsley


Includes:

Maintenance Equipment (shovel, wheelbarrow, etc.)

Structural Equipment (planter boxes, wood)

Instructors: You will be taught the science of horticulture by Seed’s garden masters.

Food Preparation and Preservation

Through a collaborative partnership between Seed’s master chefs and your local community, your community will learn how to incorporate the fresh produce and herbs harvested from your garden through different food preparation and preservation processes, always ensuring to complement the diverse cultures of your communities.

On-going Support

You will receive online and in-person support from the day you sign up to our platform. From working with your community through fundraising opportunities to providing technical support should you experience any difficulties on our platform, we will continually work with you to ensure a seamless experience.
