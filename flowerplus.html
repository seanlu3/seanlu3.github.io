Flowerplus

Digital Implementation Guide

Objective: Design a better user experience and user interface.

Vision: Create a better UX and UI to attract website visitors, to let the user easily to find what flowers they want, as well as a simple step check out for ordering flowers.

Scenario: Donna is looking for some flowers to decorate her front porch. She went to the superstore nearby, but they didn’t carry a lot selections. So she decides to go online and looks for it. However, she does not have much experience with shopping online.

Digital Principle: a remarkable clean looking flower shopping website with very brief helping guide throughout the entire site.
